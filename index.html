Vivid in Japanese art and imagination are creatures that are at once ghastly and amusing


Yökai is a catchall word for ghosts, demons, monsters, shapeshifters, tricksters, and other kinds on super natural beings and mysterious phenomena. Yökai interact with the human world and spark common notions of frightful things
